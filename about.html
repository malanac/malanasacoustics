Hi, I'm Malana!

I am a singer and play the piano, guitar, and ukulele. My musical journey began in 2015 as I toured seven countries in Europe with the Michigan Ambassadors of Music. During my freshman year at the University of Michigan, I joined the Women's Glee Club and performed in talent shows.

I started performing cover and original songs on Instagram to motivate and empower others throughout this COVID-19 era. Songs that I've covered include OneRepublic, Camila Cabello, and Khalid.

When I'm not singing or playing the piano, you can find me watching dramas and eating ice cream.

Check out my Instagram account, Malana's Acoustics! You can DM me for inquiries or go to the "Contact Me" page and fill out a form.
