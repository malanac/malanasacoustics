Hi, I'm Malana!

I am a singer and play the piano, guitar, and ukulele. My musical journey began in 2015 as I toured seven countries in Europe with the Michigan Ambassadors of Music. During my freshman year at the University of Michigan, I joined the Women's Glee Club and performed in talent shows.

I started performing cover and original songs on Instagram to motivate and empower others throughout this COVID-19 era. Artists whose songs I've covered include OneRepublic, Camila Cabello, and Khalid.

Check out my Instagram account, Malana's Acoustics! I cover songs on the piano, guitar, and ukulele. I also craft my own songs and collaborated with JCProductions where our song can be found on Spotify. You can DM me for inquiries or go to the "Contact Me" page and fill out a form.
