MALANA CHAN

Hi, I'm Malana!

I am currently a junior at the University of Michigan - School of Information. I am majoring in UX Design and minoring in Asian Languages and Cultures, Chinese.

As a first generation college student, the educational and financial obstacles I've faced have shaped me to become self-motivated, communicative, and resourceful.

Channeling these characteristics along with my experiences in Management and Operations, I am an empathetic designer. I am dedicated to designing accessible and inclusive products to improve user experiences.

Outside of school, I express my passion for music through my Instagram account, Malana's Acoustics.
